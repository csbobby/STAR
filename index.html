What is STAR?

Reasoning in the real-world is not divorced from situations.
-- a key challenge is to capture the knowledge from surrounding situations dynamically and reason accordingly.

We introduce a challenging video question answering benchmark to measure machine intelligence on situated reasoning, called Situated Reasoning in Real-World Videos (STAR). This benchmark is built upon real-world videos with human actions that are naturally dynamic and compositional. We hope it will provide an enabling resource for situated reasoning from real-world videos.

Preview

In this dataset, situations are decomposed by bottom-up hyper-graphs with atomic elements (e. g., actions, objects, and relationships). Let's look at one of the examples below...

sdfsdf

Questions are procedurally generated using functional programs based on the situation hyper-graphs. The dataset includes four types of questions, including interaction, sequence, prediction, and feasibility.
